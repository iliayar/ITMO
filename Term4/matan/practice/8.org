#+begin_solution org

- \(x = 1,\ r\cos\varphi\cos\psi = 1\)
- \(r = 0,\ \psi = 0\)
- \(y = 0,\ r\sin\varphi\cos\psi = 0,\ \phi = 0\)

#+begin_solution org
\-
- \(x = ar\cos\varphi\cos\psi\)
- \(y = ar\sin\varphi\cos\psi\)

\[ r = \sqrt[3]{\tg\psi} \]

#+end_solution

\[ w = \ln w - \ln v \Rightarrow v = e^{\ln w - w} = \frac{w}{e^w}  \]
\[ V = abc \int_0^1 dw \int_0^w du \int\limits_{we^{-w}}^w 1 dv \]
#+end_solution
