|---------+-----------------+-----------------+-------------------+-----------+---------------------------------------------+-----------|
| \(p\)   | \(\frac{1}{2}\) | \(\frac{1}{4}\) | \(\frac{1}{8}\)   | \(\dots\) | \(\frac{1}{2^n}\)                           | \(\dots\) |
\[ E\xi = 1 \cdot \sum_{n = 1}^\infty \frac{1}{2^n} = \frac{\frac{1}{2}}{1 - \frac{1}{2}} = 1 \]
